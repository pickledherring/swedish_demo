Sing with us

Rehearsals every Monday evening at seven, at the Swedish Club. No audition, no Swedish required, only a willingness to learn. Arrive fifteen minutes early to meet the section leaders. Sopranos, altos, tenors, and basses are all welcome, and the director can help place new singers on the spot. Most new members are ready for the stage within one season, with sheet music and folders provided.
